FAQ

1. What if our DJ is sick?

In case of illness, DJ og Event fortunately has a large network that we can draw on and therefore, with DJ og Event you are assured that a skilled dj will come and play at your planned event.

2. Can we cancel the DJ?

At Dj Og Event, we are aware that situations where an event is cancelled. There are conditions of cancelling that must be followed: - If the cancellation of the DJ is confirmed by us 14 days before the event date, the cancellation will be free of charge. - If the cancellation occurs between 7-13 days before the event date, 50% of the full amount must be paid. - If the cancellation occurs between 0-6 days before the event date, then the full amount must be paid.

3. When does the DJ arrive at the event?

Unless otherwise agreed, the DJ arrives approx. 1 hour before the DJ has to start playing. The actual setup and preparation of the DJ's equipment takes approx 30-40 minutes

4. Can we request songs from the DJ?

Of course, you have ample opportunity to request numbers for your party. At DJ og Event our most important task is to have a good evening. You have 3 options to request music tracks and music genres for the evening: - In the e-mail sent to you with the order confirmation for our agreement, you will find a link to an "Expectation poll". Here you can write down wishes for music in the evening, which means something to you. You also have the opportunity to note whether you have chosen a specific theme for the evening. - When the DJ contacts you, you can talk about desired songs and music genres. Should it be 80s and 90s music or something completely different, you can mention it here. - When the DJ plays, he will of course also accept wishes. The DJ will try to play the desired tracks to suit the mood of the time.
